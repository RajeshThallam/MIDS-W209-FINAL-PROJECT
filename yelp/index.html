  • Sept 2015

    Idea

    Our project is to explore and communicate insights from Yelp's businesses, users, reviews and check-ins dataset. Our idea is to perform time-series analysis and look at the rating trends for a business over time

  • Goal

    The goal of the visualization is to help business understand their performance and rating in the community and show them competitive analysis of other businesses in the same category. If a business is suddenly experiencing a downturn, they can look at the sentiments of what users are expressing in reviews and take that as an indicator to take corrective action.

  • Data

    The data for the project is taken from Yelp dataset challenge

  • Tools

    D3, DC, Elastic Search, AWS
